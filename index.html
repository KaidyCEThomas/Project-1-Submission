Cocoa Butter


  • Prevents Skin Dryness and Peeling - Cocoa butter makes an excellent skin moisturizer

  • Fights Signs of Aging - Cocoa butter contains compounds called cocoa mass polyphenols,
    which can help diminish signs of aging

  • Helps Improve Heart Health - The polyphenolic components found in cocoa butter
    have been shown to help lower inflammatory markers involved in atherosclerosis

    • Shea Butter

  • Sun Protection - Shea butter may not replace your sunscreen, but it can provide
    some protection against UV exposure.

  • Anti-inflammatory - Shea butter contains several anti-inflammatory agents such as cinnamic acid,
    known to reduce skin inflammations.

  • Anti-aging - The fatty acid profile of the butter allows it to be absorbed
    into the deeper skin layers to nourish them and support collagen production.

    • Coconut Oil

  • Skincare - Coconut oil is excellent massage oil that acts as an effective moisturizer
    on all types of skin, including dry skin.

  • Hair care - Coconut oil helps in healthy growth of hair and gives your hair a shiny quality.
    It is also highly effective in reducing protein loss

  • Weight loss - Coconut oil is very useful for weight loss.
    It contains short and medium-chain fatty acids that help in taking off excessive weight.
